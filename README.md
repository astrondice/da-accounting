## 🏛️ Firm Overview

Located in Rajpura, Punjab, **D.A Accounting Services** is a premier fiscal and legal consultancy firm dedicated to empowering businesses, traders, and individual professionals. Led by Deepanshu Ahuja, our practice brings extensive hands-on expertise in navigating India's complex taxation and regulatory compliance landscape.

Our mission is to unburden entrepreneurs from tedious paperwork and legal compliance hurdles, enabling them to focus entirely on scaling their enterprises with peace of mind.

## 💼 Core Competencies

We offer end-to-end management of business registrations, regulatory compliance, and taxation affairs:

*   **Taxation:** Complete GST Registration & Return Filing, Income Tax Return (ITR) Preparation and Assessment.
*   **Business Licensing:** Udyam (MSME) Registration, FSSAI (FoSCoS) Food Licensing.
*   **Corporate Compliance:** EPFO (PF) & ESIC Registration, Corporate PAN & TAN Issuance.
*   **Certification & Security:** ISO Certification Assistance, Digital Signature Certificate (DSC) Provisioning.
*   **Financial Administration:** Comprehensive Bookkeeping, Ledger Management, and Accounting Services.

## ⚖️ Professional Standards & Legal Compliance

D.A Accounting Services operates under strict adherence to the prevailing financial statutes and legal frameworks of India. Our practice is built on:

- **100% Transparency:** Clear, upfront disclosures on fees and legal procedures with no hidden costs.
- **Data Confidentiality:** Stringent professional protocols to protect sensitive corporate, financial, and personal client data.
- **Regulatory Accuracy:** Continuous monitoring of amendments in GST laws, Income Tax Acts, and MSME guidelines to guarantee precise and timely filings.
